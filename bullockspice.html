Bullock Spice

Project: Identity & Web - UI/UX

Bullock Spice is a study of a company that focuses on providing high quality and a wide range of high quality single and blended spices through a user intuitive website and app layout. The advance of this company is the high-quality spices and the nowledgeable staff offering advice on spices that are mailed direct to your house.

Design Thinking |Define problem

Understanding the struggle with finding that one spice or spice blend: a simpler and easier way to find and purchase spices is needed.

Design Thinking |Ideate

Sharing and exploring ideas that have meaningful value and following the convergence and divergence process of how one comes to find a spice for the end user. After this, prioritizing what is most important and seeing if there is room for added value or benefit.

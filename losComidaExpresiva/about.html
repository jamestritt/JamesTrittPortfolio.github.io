~About our company~

Los Comida Expresiva translates to The expressive food. Chef Erin, who was born deaf and raised in Guatemala until the age of eight when she immigrated to the San Jose area. After working in different jobs, she started to hang around more Mexican style restaurants she started to think about how she could share her love of Guatemala food and culture to people. After one night working, she talked to Mark and the two of them opened Los Comida Experiva food truck. Los Comida Expersiva is all about sharing, connecting with people and creating memories.

Chef: Steve

Steve has been working on different food trucks around the San Jose area. His intrested in working on a food truck started with late night visit at food tuck after a night out. He is the workhorse behind the scene.

Chef: Mark

After working many years in fine French dinning restaurants, and Hotels, Mark connected with Erin and had an idea to start up a Guatemala food truck.

Chef: Erin

She is the brains behind Las Comida Expresiva. After leaving, Guatemala, she opened a food truck to share and expressive her love and passion of Guatemala food and culture to people.

Chef: Levi

Chef Levi has worked in many different Mexican style restaurants in the San Jose area and different parts of Mexico. He brings vast knowledge, skill and personality that makes a rememberable experience

Anti Stain Shirt

Project: Web Banner Add

Created and designed two web banners that would be displayed as a web banner advert for a new Anti Staining dress shirt. These adverts were developed to promote the Anti Staining dress shirt and grab the viewers attention through animation and direct them to where they can purchase through at CTA.

Web Banner - 720 x 300px

Web Banner - 300 x 600px

The Max-Anti Stain: the dress shirt that is made and tailored towards working professionals. This shirt showcases its features of repealing liquids like coffee, soda, beer and water by having hydrophobic cotton and anti-stain properties blended into the fabric. Whether you travel for business, work from home or like having the peace of mind of not staining your shirt, then this shirt is for you.

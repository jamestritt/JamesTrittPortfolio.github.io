Hi!

I have always been fascinated with the relationship of art and science. Whereas science is logical, analytical and done in a step by step process, art is the creative, expressive and the organic process of creating and connecting with people. Over the years, I have found that they can work together to bring beautiful works that enrich our lives. I set out to marry these two ideas together of being visually pleasing to look at and being useful that make our life or lives more meaningful and purposeful.

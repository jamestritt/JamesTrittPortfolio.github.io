Process Work

The work below show the stages of the desing process of Los Comida Expresiva food truck.

Brainstorming

Using the clown theme, coming up with different ideas that would create an interesting product or service. Coming up with a list of these ideas, I took inspiration from what is already existing, what would be interesting/forward thinking and juxtaposition. This inspiration covered products from makeup and clothing to companies providing a service like car rental or house cleaning all the way to a food truck concept.

  • Brainstorming List

Design Research

After coming up with fifty different themes that would based around clowns, the final theme would be working in a food truck as the general theme. After researching the many different types of clowns and their subgroups and understanding what each of them represented, the clown type of mime was picked with a focus of using hand gestures to communicate with customers. Using the foundational mime makeup starting point and combining day of the dead face design, this clown makeup style contains a white base true to mime makeup with highlights and accents of day of the dead colors and imagery to complete the clown look.

Mock Ups

Having a food truck and allowing its customer to easily and effortlessly use the website is critical for having low user fatigue. The key design features of this website are having bold, bright and colorful imagery, clean and easy to read food options, CTA's and finding out where the food truck is for the day.

Personal

Knowing who the end user is and what they are looking for in a food trucks that enters in their communities and offers them options for a quick meal. The major findings revealed variety/customization, history/sense of connection and social responsibility are important factors for eating at a food truck.
